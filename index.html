Gender Equality Tracker: Towards a More Equal World

Data Source: United Nations Development Programme, The World Bank.

Data Updated: 2021

Welcome to the Gender Equality Tracker Dashboard. This dashboard serves as a comprehensive tool for understanding and monitoring progress towards gender equality in six countries: Brazil, Canada, China, Germany, India, and the United States. Through a lens that transcends borders and cultures, we aim to shed light on the status of gender equity, providing valuable insights, trends, and data-driven narratives to inform policy decisions, inspire change, and promote inclusivity. You can also access the data used to create this dashboard by clicking the Data Source in the navigation bar.
